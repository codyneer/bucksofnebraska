Our Story

Bucks of Nebraska didn't start in a boardroom. It started in the timber. In the cold. In those early mornings when the only sound is your own breath and the crunch of frost under your boots.

We're hunters first. We grew up chasing whitetails across the Cornhusker State ‚Äî from the river bottoms of eastern Nebraska to the sandhills out west. And we wanted gear that reflected that life.

More Than a Logo

Every design comes from a real experience. The antler "N" isn't just a logo ‚Äî it's a symbol of what it means to be a Nebraska hunter. It represents the land, the tradition, and the community.

Nebraska Proud

We believe in supporting our state, our communities, and the conservation efforts that keep Nebraska's hunting heritage alive. A portion of every sale goes toward wildlife conservation in Nebraska.
